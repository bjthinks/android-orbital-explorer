What is this?

This app shows you the shapes of electron orbitals. An orbital is the volume of space that a single electron occupies in an atom.

What are the numbers?

First is N, the energy level or shell number. Second is L, the orbital angular momentum, and third is M, the z-projection of angular momentum. The ℂ or ℝ selects from two different sets of basis functions.
